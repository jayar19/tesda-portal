About

The Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) provides direction, policies, programs, and standards toward quality technical education and skills development. TESDA Region VII - Dumaguete City Provincial Office is committed to empowering individuals through skills training, assessment, certification, and scholarship opportunities.
